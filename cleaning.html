How It Works

1

Book Your Service

Schedule a pickup online or through website.

2

Items Recieved

Your articles are safely received by our team through convenient methods such as doorstep pickup, courier, or in-store drop-off. Rest assured, they are in good hands from the moment they reach us.

3

Inspection

Our meticulous team thoroughly inspects your articles, carefully assessing any damages or necessary work. Based on our findings, we recommend suitable services with transparent pricing, ensuring you are well-informed throughout the process.

4

Estimate Approval

Once you confirm the desired services, we provide a final estimate for your approval. Only after estimate and service approval are received, your articles are sent to our workshop, ready to undergo their transformation.

5

Work in Progress

Your articles embark on their restoration journey at our dedicated workshop. Our skilled technicians employ their expertise to deliver the specific services required, ensuring attention to detail and impeccable craftsmanship.

6

Quality Check

Our dedicated quality assurance team examines each article, ensuring that the services have been impeccably completed. We strive for excellence at every stage, upholding our commitment to delivering exceptional results.

7

Photo Approval

We value your satisfaction. Before finalizing the process, we share pictures of the completed work with you. You have the opportunity to review and provide feedback. If any concerns arise, we're here to address them promptly, ensuring your complete satisfaction.

8

Packing

With utmost care, our specialized packing team ensures that each item is packed using high-grade materials. Additionally, we provide FreshFeets dustbags to safeguard your items, ensuring they are protected during transit.

9

Payment

To streamline the process, we share an invoice with you for convenient payment. We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit cards, debit cards, and online payment gateways.

10

Delivery

Finally, your transformed articles are ready for collection or delivery as per your preference. We accommodate your needs, ensuring a seamless and satisfying experience from start to finish.
